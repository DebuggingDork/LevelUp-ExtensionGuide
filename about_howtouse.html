Floating Menu for Instant Meanings

Select any word and get comprehensive definitions, examples, and contextual meanings through an elegant floating menu.

  1. 1
    Select Any Word: Highlight any word or phrase you want to understand on any webpage
  2. 2
    Floating Menu Appears: A floating menu bar will automatically appear near your selection
  3. 3
    Click Meaning Icon: Click on the icon in the floating menu
  4. 4
    View Comprehensive Results: Get definition, synonyms, usage examples, and sentences with the word
  5. 5
    Add Context (Optional): Provide additional context to get the exact meaning in your specific situation
  6. 6
    Save to Vocabulary: Click "Add to Vocabulary" to save the word for later review
